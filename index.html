Look and See Program

This program is tailor-made to suit the expat individual needs. Detailed information on various aspects of expat life in the designated city is presented to allow the expats and family to become familiar with the customs and culture and to identify any potential issues before officially accepting the assignment in India.

Home Search Program

Eximius Mobility specializes in the pre-screening of suitable properties as per the requirements to ensure that expats have the greatest possible market access at the time of their relocation to India. This guarantees that time is spent viewing only the properties which most closely match the expat’s personal needs and requirements.

Immigration Services

  • Employment Visa, Dependent Visa and Business Visa in the Home Country
  • FRRO Registration formalities
  • Change of address at the FRRO
  • Return Permit, Exit Permit, New Passport
  • Visa Extension
  • Visa application for a new born baby in India (Expatriate)
  • Police Verification / Clearance formalities

School Search Program

Eximius Mobility will schedule appointments with the international schools. Our relocation consultant will accompany you to the school and the school admission concerned person will conduct a school tour.

Assistance with Temporary Accommodation

  • Understanding expats needs and provide temporary accommodation options
  • Provide list of temporary accommodation as per expats requirements
  • Negotiation of cost
  • Assistance with the check in and settling in

Repatriation Program

Upon completion of your assignment in India, there will no doubt once again be many things to think about and keep you busy. EMS’s comprehensive repatriation program takes care of all of the necessary closures in Bengaluru, allowing you to focus on wrapping up things at work and preparing for a smooth return to your home country.

Settling - In Services

Once the expat move in to the property, we assist them with utilities hook ups:

  • Internet connection
  • Satellite TV connection
  • Cooking gas connection
  • Assistance with bank account opening

Maneuvering

  • Furniture purchase / rental
  • Car rental / purchase
  • Assistance with Maids and Drivers
  • Social engagements
